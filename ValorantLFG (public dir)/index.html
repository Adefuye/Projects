What is VALORANT LFG

Valorant LFG is a platform to help Valorant players find squads. If you're a casual player looking for chill fun matches, competitive player looking for serious Ranked players with experience, or a streamer looking for other streamers and players to join your stream, simply make a post and start building your squad.

To make a post, it's simple. Input your Riot Games username, what region you're from, and what type of players you're looking for, and that's it, you're all set!

The rules for posts are simple, and should be easy to follow:
- No bullying
- No sexism
- No racism
- No homophobia
- or other hate-based comments
